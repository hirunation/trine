# Vision

TRINE optimizes for determinism, cost discipline, and structural honesty. Every design decision serves a single constraint: the system must be correct before it is fast, and fast before it is featureful. No capability is added that cannot be verified, no default is chosen that trades safety for convenience.

The project honors a long horizon. Infrastructure that becomes invisible earns that position through years of zero surprises, not through clever marketing or rapid iteration. TRINE is built to be boring — predictable, auditable, and unchanged by the pressures of novelty.

Engineering discipline means saying no to most things. The 270-byte algebraic core, the zero-dependency C99 contract, the deterministic output guarantee — these are not limitations. They are the architecture. Restraint is the feature.
